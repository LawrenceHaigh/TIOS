About Us

We offer a complete range of Light Well Intervention services to the global market

TIOS has a track record of servicing more than 600 subsea wells, resulting in significant increased production from most of them.

TIOS (formerly Island Offshore Subsea and jointly owned by TechnipFMC and Island Offshore) is the Group´s globally exclusive entity to perform vessel based Light Well Intervention (LWI) services:

  • Riserless wireline based intervention (RLWI)
  • Riserless coiled tubing (RLCT)
  • Plug and abandonment (P&A)
  • Pilot hole / top hole drilling
  • Downhole technology

The above well intervention services are very competence driven. In addition to committed, highly skilled and experienced offshore personnel, we have the following resources onshore to efficiently execute and plan the operations:

  • Project management resources
  • Engineering department
  • HSE/QA and risk management resources
  • Offshore Operational management

In addition to the above, we will utilize our mother companies on a “need to have” basis, enabling us to pull highly competent resources into any issues related to either vessel or well control equipment.

Services

Worldwide there are more than 6000 subsea wells, and the number is increasing. These wells are getting older, resulting in the need of well interventions to maintain production or integrity.

Traditionally, a drilling rig performs the well intervention. We perform Light Well Intervention (LWI) services from a monohull vessel.

Comparing these two methods, our intervention services provide:

  • Lower carbon footprint per operation
  • Improved operational efficiency per well
  • Cost reduction per day

Riserless Wireline based Intervention (RLWI)
For subsea wells, all services done from a rig with riserbased wireline, we can do from a vessel with riserless wireline. This may include diagnostics, stimulation, surveillance, zonal isolation, manipulation of equipment and repair of mechanical failure.

Riserless Coiled Tubing (RLCT)
TIOS has developed a riserless coiled tubing concept for operations where a normal wireline service does not have enough force, and/or you need to circulate.

Plug & Abandonment (P&A)
The last few years, monohull vessels have successfully plugged and abandoned subsea wells and recovered wellheads, jobs traditionally done by drilling rigs. TIOS complies with Norwegian and UK rules and demands for P&A, and can supply full project responsibility.

Pilot hole/ Top hole drilling
Based on several studies and two successful pilot hole operations, TIOS now provides the capability to drill pilot holes, drill surface hole for conductor pipe, install the subsea wellhead system and cement the pipe in place.

Downhole Technology
Through Research & Development (R&D) projects and experienced personnel, TIOS develops new and improved downhole tools missing from the market. Our goal is further improvement of our services. We are now launching the Precision Drilling Tool (PDT), designed to open for production. To market reservoir stimulation technology, TIOS partially owns and cooperates with a Canadian company, Penetrators Canada Inc.

Engineering services
We provide integrated engineering services to plan, design and manage solutions for complex projects in harsh and challenging environments. We perform studies in close cooperation with clients to clarify methods and potential scope of work. Through Research & Development (R&D) projects, TIOS continuously works to extend the life of the fields, increase efficiency and reduce costs.

Onshore operational support
To support the offshore operations, we have an onshore operation department of highly skilled persons. Long experience and high competence in project management, Light Well Intervention operations and marine operation is one of our strengths.

Offshore personnel
Our offshore personnel are our most valuable asset. Committed, highly skilled and experienced, we perform our operations with pride. Every year we improve the operation efficiency and continuously strive to perform safe operations with no injuries. We constantly challenge and improve “best practice” in everything we do!

Track record and work experience
TIOS has intervened more than 600 subsea wells. In total, we have performed more than 3300 descents into a well, which includes a significant range of services.

TIOS currently operates four fully integrated LWI systems on a global scale.

  • Island Frontier + Stack #1, 2005
  • Island Wellserver + Stack #2, 2009
  • Island Constructor + Stack #3, 2009
  • Island Performer + Stack #4, 2016
