Our employees are our most valuable asset.

TIOS onshore offices are located on Forus outside Stavanger, Norway and in Aberdeen, UK. We employ about 50 people onshore and 100 offshore. We offer long-term job and development opportunities.

Personnel policy
It is of great importance that our work and operations are executed with the utmost consideration to the health and safety of our workers and the environment. Safety always comes first.

Our employees are our most valuable assets and the quality of our services is the result of a joined effort to exceed our customersâ€™ expectations. TIOS offers a variety of competence development and career opportunities both offshore and onshore. Our goal is to offer all personnel the opportunity to develop their personal and professional skills.

TIOS also seeks to develop and maintain close relationships between our offshore crew and the onshore organization. This focus is essential to our strategy and the future development of our company. We strive to be a caretaker for our employees and their families.
